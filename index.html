What is good design?

To me, good design is when there are many interesting aspects to an image, but not too many that they become overwhelming. I like when many parts of a design can easily flow together to create something aesthetically pleasing to the eye. My favorite images are ones that are put together in a way that invokes some kind of emotion or tone, whether it be nostalgia, calmness, or inspiration. I tend to gravitate towards images that inspire a sense of adventure, leading the viewer to believe there is more in the scene than what they are immediately seeing.
